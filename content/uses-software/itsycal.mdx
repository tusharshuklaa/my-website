[Itsycal](https://www.mowgli.com/) is a tiny calendar widget for Mac developed by Mowgli.

### Why Itsycal?

Itsycal is designed to be a lightweight and unobtrusive calendar solution for Mac users. It integrates seamlessly with the macOS menu bar, providing quick access to your calendar events and reminders without taking up valuable screen space.

Itsycal is available exclusively for macOS.
