[Notion](https://www.notion.so/) is an all-in-one workspace developed by Notion Labs Inc.

### Why Notion?

Notion is my go-to tool for organizing my thoughts, tasks, and projects. It serves as my all-in-one workspace for task management and note-taking.

Free tier covers most needs; paid plans add collaboration features. Notion is available on multiple platforms, including Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android.
