[HiddenBar](https://gethiddenbar.com/) is a menu bar application developed for macOS.

### Why HiddenBar?

HiddenBar helps me declutter my Mac's menu bar by hiding unnecessary widgets and icons. I can toggle visibility when needed while maintaining a clean workspace. Simple drag-and-drop organization keeps only essential items visible.

It's completely free and incredibly lightweight. HiddenBar is available on macOS.
