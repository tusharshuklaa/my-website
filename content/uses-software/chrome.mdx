[Google Chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/) is a fast, secure, and versatile web browser developed by Google.

### Why Google Chrome?

It offers a wide range of features including tabbed browsing, synchronization across devices, and a vast library of extensions.
The browser is built on the Blink rendering engine and is known for its speed and efficiency.
Google Chrome is available on multiple platforms, including Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android.

- Firefox
- Microsoft Edge
- Safari
