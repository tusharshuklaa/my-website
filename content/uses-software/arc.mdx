[Arc Browser](https://arc.net/) is a modern web browser developed by The Browser Company.

### Why Arc Browser?

It offers a unique approach to browsing with features like a split-view layout, built-in tools for note-taking and organization, and a focus on collaboration.
The browser is designed to enhance productivity and streamline workflows for users.

Arc Browser is available on multiple platforms, including Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android.
